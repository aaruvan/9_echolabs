{# --- Method note (statistical thinking) --- #}

Statistical thinking

  • Distributions (SP-1): histogram exposes skew that a mean would hide.
  • Group comparison (SP-3, C-3): bucketing by segment count and duration tests whether the AI scales consistently with input size.
  • Trend over time (UB-1, C-2): a rolling weekly view smooths noise and surfaces real growth or drift.
  • Proportions vs. counts (UB-3): the donut adds a relative view alongside the raw counts.
  • Cost proxy (C-1, C-2, C-3): word count is used as a token-count proxy — relative comparisons stay valid even without billing data.
